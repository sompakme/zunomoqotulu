    CM Punk suffered triceps injury at the Royal Rumble, casting doubt on his participation in WrestleMania 40. Unfortunately, he will miss many events because of that, but that doesn’t mean fans will forget him. In fact, Punk and his dog Larry got their own personalized action figures from Zombie Sailor’s Toys.

    A new CM Punk action figure is now open for pre-order, featuring a special bonus. Zombie Sailor’s Toys revealed their collaboration with CM Punk for the figure.

    In the announcement video, CM Punk mentioned that if he is part of the Wrestling’s Heels and Faces collection, his dog Larry will accompany him.

    The CM Punk figure, featuring a bonus Larry figure, is currently open for pre-order on Zombie Sailor’s website. Estimated to ship in late summer or early fall, this collectible is made-to-order and will be available for approximately two weeks. Following this period, the figure will no longer be offered.

    WWE announced this week that CM Punk had successful triceps surgery as well. Punk is also being eyed for commentary role while he remains absent from WWE television. We will have to wait and see what WWE will have planned for Punk once he makes his triumphant in-ring return.
