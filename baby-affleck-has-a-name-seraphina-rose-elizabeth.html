    Baby Affleck Has A Name: Seraphina Rose Elizabeth

    One week after Jennifer Garner gave birth to her second daughter, the actress and hubby Ben Affleck have revealed the name of the newest addition to their family. She is Seraphina Rose Elizabeth Affleck.

    27 Aug 2024, 00:00

    One week after Jennifer Garner gave birth to her second daughter, the actress and hubby Ben Affleck have revealed the name of the newest addition to their family. She is Seraphina Rose Elizabeth Affleck.

    The couple, who married in 2005, have another daughter, three year old Violet Anne Affleck.

    What’s in a name? Hollywood celebrities have a habit of picking names that fall outside of the norm.

    Jessica Alba and Cash Warren named their daughter Honor. Gwen Stefani named her boys Kingston and Zuma Nesta Rock. Gwyneth Paltrow chose Apple and Moses. David Duchovny named his child Kyd. Jenna Elfman named her child Story. Nicole Richie and Joel Madden named their daughter Harlow Winter Kate.

    Demi Moore and Bruce Willis named their girls Rumer, Scout and Tallulah Belle. Frank Zappa chose Moon Unit, Dweezel and Diva Thin Muffin. You get the idea…

    So what the heck was new mom Alison Sweeney thinking when she gave her baby daughter a conventional name?
