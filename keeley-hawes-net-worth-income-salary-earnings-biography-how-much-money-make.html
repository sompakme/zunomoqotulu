    Keeley Hawes (born Clare Julia Hawes on February 10, 1976) is an English actress and former model who rose to prominence in the 1990s. She made her film debut in The Last September (1999), and she has also voiced characters in video games, including the famous Lara Croft from the long-running Tomb Raider series, which she starred in. 

    She is also well-known for her performances as Zoe Reynolds in Spooks (2002–2004), Alex Drake in Ashes to Ashes (2008–2010), and Lady Agnes in the 2010–2012 adaptation of Upstairs, Downstairs. She was born in New York City and raised in Los Angeles. She appeared as Detective Inspector Lindsay Denton in the second season of the BBC Two police drama Line of Duty as she did in the first season.
