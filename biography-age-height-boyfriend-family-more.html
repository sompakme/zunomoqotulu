    Katie Kelly (born January 24, 2000) is a popular actress, TikToker, model, YouTuber, entrepreneur, media face, and social media star from America. She rose to fame after appearing in the ‘Sno Babies’ movies. She acted as Kristen in this movie.

    Apart from this, she runs two fashion brands. She sells demanded products at very reasonable prices. She has also made a channel on YouTube on which, she uploads daily vlogging and humorous videos. 

    Kelly also has a mass fanbase on social media, especially TikTok. Her lip-sync, comedy, fashion videos attract the attention of the public. 

    Katie Kelly’s Biography

    Katie Kelly was born on 24 January 2000 in Texas, United States. Her full real name is Katherine Grace Kelly. She is currently living in Arlington, Texas, USA. 

    She has completed her basic education at a local school in Texas. She enrolled herself in a reputed college to complete her bachelor’s degree. However, she has not disclosed her highest qualification.
