    The Purging Monument delivers a variety of services to the player based on their current condition in a manner similar to that of Velka’s monument in the Undead Settlement. These services include: Request Absolution: This function allows the player to be forgiven of their crimes by another player. If they have not committed a sin, a message will appear informing them of this.

    What does Argo do when he is resurrected in a similar manner?

    It is possible to resurrect the defeated Argo and his champion at the Purging Monument if the player has a certain number of souls to spend on him. Their return to the covenant will allow them to level up their status within it while reentering the church will make him hostile once more.

    How do you open the door in ringed city streets?

    You’ll be able to unlock the shortcut door (the path to the monument is right beside the shortcut door) just by progressing through the area. To be more specific, it’s past the part in the swamp that has all of the ringed knights.
