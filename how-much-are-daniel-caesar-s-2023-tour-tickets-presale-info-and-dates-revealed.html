    How much are Daniel Caesars 2023 tour tickets? Presale info and dates revealed

    Daniel Caesar recently announced the Superpower tours dates for North America and Canada and fans are already wondering about the presale and how much his tickets cost.     25 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Daniel Caesar recently announced the Superpower tour’s dates for North America and Canada and fans are already wondering about the presale and how much his tickets cost.

    Canadian singer Daniel Caesar is going on a world tour in 2023. The musician had previously announced the tour dates for the Asia leg of his Superpowers tour.

    He has now released an extensive dates list for his North America and Canadian concerts.

    The 28-year-old singer recently released his 2023 album Never Enough which features hit tracks like Toronto 2014, Buyer’s Remorse, Unstoppable, Homiesexual, and more.

    Daniel Caesar’s Superpower tour’s presale explored

    Daniel’s North America and Canada tickets go on sale on Friday, June 16. However, fans who wish to get their hands on the tickets early can participate in the presale.

    There are several presale options available for fans all of which will begin in the upcoming dates. Here’s a list of all of them with timings.

    • Daniel Caesar Socials Artist Presale: Starts on June 13 at 10:00 am EDT and ends on June 15 at 10:00 pm EDT.
    • Daniel Caesar Super Fan Artist Presale: Starts on June 13 at 10:00 am EDT and ends on June 15 at 10:00 pm EDT.
    • Official Platinum: Starts on June 13 at 10:00 am EDT and ends on August 30 at 09:00 pm EDT.
    • Live Nation Presale: Starts on June 14 at 10:00 am EDT and ends on June 15 at 10:00 pm EDT.
    • HOB Foundation Room Member Presale: June 14 at 10:00 am EDT to June 15 at 10:00 pm EDT (exclusive to a few venues)
    • Spotify Presale: Starts on June 14 at 10:00 am EDT to June 15 at 10:00 pm EDT.
    • Ticketmaster Presale: Starts on June 14 at 10:00 am EDT to June 15 at 10:00 pm EDT.
    • Waterloo Greenway Venue Presale: June 14 at 10:00 am EDT to June 15 at 10:00 pm EDT (exclusive to a few venues)

    How much are Daniel Caesar’s world tour tickets?

    Those who do not wish to wait for the presale or the on-sale can head on sites like Seat Geek, Vivid Seats, StubHub, and Ticketsmarter.

    At the time of writing, the tickets start at a low price of $127 on Seat Geek. On Vivid Seats too they start at the low price of $127 but can go up to $1000 if not more.

    Over on Stubhuub, they have a starting price of $138. On Ticketsmarter they are priced at $129.

    Daniel’s North America shows will also feature special guests Charlotte Day Wilson, BADBADNOTGOOD, Flying Lotus, Montell Fish, Moses Sumney, Omar Apollo, and Orion Sun.
