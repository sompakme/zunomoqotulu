    14 Aug 2024, 00:00

    WWE brought quite a show for NXT this week, as they aired the second night of their two-week Halloween Havoc event. Now, we know how many viewers tuned in.

    Ringside News provided complete coverage of NXT Halloween Havoc Night 2. The two-hour show went fast due to the number of eclectic matches they presented, and the show trended #1 on X during the broadcast.

    SportsTVRatings reports that the October 31st episode of NXT brought in 674,000 viewers for their Halloween Havoc Night 2 event. They also pulled in a .20 in the 18 to 49 demographic. That number was down a bit from the previous week.

    The October 24th episode of WWE NXT brought in 787,000 viewers and scored a .21 rating among 18-49 year olds for their Halloween Havoc Night 1 special. The main event of that night saw Becky Lynch lose her NXT Women’s Title to Lyra Valkyria.

    NXT Halloween Havoc night 2 on October 31, 2023, featured a loaded card of intense showdowns. Although one match didn’t happen at all, because Tiffany Stratton jumped Fallon Henley before the match even started, they still delivered a ton of action.

    In a Tables, Ladders, and ‘Scares’ Match, the Creed Brothers secured a victory over Angel Garza and Humberto Carrillo. Dominik Mysterio, the NXT North American Champion, successfully defended his title, defeating Nathan Frazier.

    Bron Breakker emerged victorious in a bout against Mr. Stone, adding another win to his impressive NXT career. Chelsea Green and Piper Niven retained the WWE Women’s Tag Team Championship as they defeated Jacy Jayne and Thea Hail.

    The NXT Women’s Breakout Tournament featured a face-off between Lola Vice and Kelani Jordan, with Lola Vice emerging as the victor. In the main event, Ilja Dragunov managed to maintain his reign as NXT Champion, defeating Carmelo Hayes and retaining his coveted title.

    NXT Halloween Havoc Night 2 brought big action and unforgettable moments for wrestling fans as the NXT superstars showcased their skills and determination in the squared circle. The end of the night also ended on quite a cliffhanger, as Trick Williams returned to disrupt Carmelo Hayes’ NXT Title plans.

    We will have more on this story, and so many more, right here at Ringside News. The NXT show is continuing as WWE’s third brand, and each week it seems that they deliver even more.
