Can brain repair itself after stroke?

09 Aug 2024, 00:00

The damaged brain can recover function in many ways; all involve a process called neuroplasticity. New connections can form, allowing healthy parts of the brain to "take over" for parts that are damaged. It is even possible for new brain cells to form from stem cells in the brain. How long does it take brain to heal after stroke? The most rapid recovery usually occurs during the first three to four months after a stroke, but some survivors continue to recover well into the first and second year after their stroke.
