How much do pocket doors cost to install?

Answer Pocket Doors Are Expensive Pocket doors will cost between $500 and $1,000 when installed during a new construction project. Their cost will be in the range of $1,000 to $3,500 if they are included in a remodelling project. The prices listed here include both parts and labour. How much does it cost to install pocket doors, taking all of this into consideration? In new construction, the average cost to install a pocket door is approximately $700, while the average cost to install a pocket door in an existing wall is approximately $2,250.
