STG Completes Avid Acquisition for $1.4 Billion

An affiliate of private equity firm STG has completed the acquisition of Avid Technology — the maker of the Media Composer editing system and Pro Tools audio postproduction system, both leading tools used in Hollywood for picture editing and sound, respectively — in an all-cash transaction valued at approximately $1.4 billion. Under the terms of the agreement, Avid stockholders will receive $27.05 per share, and Avid will once again operate as a privately-held company and remain headquartered in Burlington, Massachusetts.
