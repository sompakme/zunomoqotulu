    Johnny Jett is an American craftsman, machinist, reality TV star, and painter. He is a member of the Barnwood Builders team on the television show Barnwood Builders. Besides being arguably the most popular person in the Barnwood Builders cast, he has also starred or appeared in several other Do It Yourself type TV shows. Johnny Jett is also a painter and many admirers have praised his paintings and other works of art.

    Johnny Jett bio (age)

    Johnny Jett was born on April 28, 1949 in Chicago, Illinois. Not much is known about his parents or the family of his birth. He grew up in Chicago and during his early years was very interested in machines and how they worked. A burning desire to pursue this interest led him to move to West Virginia to seek employment opportunities that would allow him to operate and work with machinery, as he could not find many in Chicago at the time. ‘era.

    He then worked in construction in the Fleming County area of ​​Kentucky. There, he also worked on the county’s water supply system. In the late 1980s and early 1990s, Jett began to seriously consider leveraging his extensive machining and construction knowledge to go into business. It was around this time that he formed a partnership with his friend Sherman Thompson. Together, they started a business specializing in the dismantling and sale of log cabins.

    After a few years in the business, Jett and Thompson met entrepreneur Mark Bowe, who introduced them to his business, Antique Cabins Barns, and they soon began working together.

    A few years after starting to work with MarkBowe, the Barnwood Builders team was formed and the eponymous reality TV show soon followed. Other cast members include Tim Rose, Graham Ferguson, Alex Webb, and Brian Buckner, who left the show after the second season. Each episode sees the crew working to restore an old barn or shack that has very often been around for over a century and would deeply affect the family or community it belongs to if it were to wither.

    Johnny Jett is considered the most popular cast member of the show due to his reputation for his creativity and craftsmanship. Despite his age, he works just as hard and often, even more so than his younger colleagues on the show. The Barnwood Builders show was hugely successful as it quickly became a fan favorite and Johnny Jett appeared in dozens of episodes.
