    The Gospel According to Google: Where in the World is Joel Houston?

    Ah, Joel Houston. The name synonymous with ripped jeans, celebrity congregants, and power ballads that make you want to raise your hands in praise (or reach for a lighter, depending on your church vibe). But what's the lead singer of Hillsong United been up to lately? Fear not, fellow seekers of the latest Christian rock news, for I, your intrepid internet sleuth, have unearthed some very important information.

    From New York Minute to Cali Dreamin':

    We all know Joel used to co-pastor Hillsong NYC alongside the now-excommunicated Carl Lentz. Let's just say their leadership styles were about as different as, well, a megachurch in Manhattan and a yoga retreat in Malibu. Remember all those headlines about Lentz's "moral failings?" Yeah, that wasn't exactly the best look for Hillsong. So, in 2017, Joel said "peace out" to the Big Apple and headed west to sunny California, presumably with a surfboard tucked under his arm.

    Still United (with a Side of Solo):

    Fear not, Hillsong United fans! Joel is still very much the frontman of this chart-topping worship band. They're constantly churning out new music that'll have you swaying in the aisles (or air guitaring in your living room). But Joel's also been hinting at a sultry solo career under the mysterious moniker "amnxnradio." Think Bruce Springsteen meets Hillsong with a dash of Bon Iver. Buckle up, because apparently deep thoughts and worship jams are about to collide.

    Is Hillsong Still a Thing?

    Well, that's a question with a complex answer. The church has certainly faced its fair share of challenges lately, but it's still a global phenomenon. As for Joel's role? He remains a big cheese in the Hillsong world, leading the creative direction of their music. So, expect to hear his fingerprints (or should we say, guitar riffs?) on future Hillsong anthems.

    The Verdict: Spreading the Gospel (and Maybe Some SoCal Vibes):

    So there you have it! Joel Houston is a busy man. He's writing worship music, teasing a solo career, and soaking up the California sun (probably). Whether you're a die-hard Hillsong fan or just curious about the latest adventures of a rockstar pastor, one thing's for sure: Joel Houston's story is far from over. Stay tuned, folks, because in the world of contemporary Christian music, there's never a dull moment.
