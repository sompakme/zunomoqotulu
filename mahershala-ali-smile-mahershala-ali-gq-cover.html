    Mahershala Ali's smile is contagious. The 43-year-old Moonlight star—and first Muslim actor to ever win an Oscar—can't stop grinning in his new cover story for GQ's July 2017 issue. A big takeaway from the shoot? He appears to thrive in the summer.

    Here he is on the cover, sitting beachside on Catalina Island, looking like the epitome of happiness with a hardly-buttoned printed shirt and beaded necklace. We imagine those rays of sunshine are actually radiating from his mega-watt smile, and that he's sipping on a tropical drink that tastes like pineapple and dreams.

    Other highlights include Mahershala in repose on a rocky beach while dressed in a suit, and Mahershala welcoming you to a sunny pier in his flip flops.
