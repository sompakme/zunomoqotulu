    Dear Frances

    Looking for a pair of block-heel sandals to add to your holiday capsule wardrobe? Opt for stacked wooden heels to create a summery, boho vibe. Crafted from Italian leather and dyed in a tangerine hue, Dear Frances' design will look particularly luxurious with crisp linen.

    Founded in 2015, Berlin-based footwear brand Aeyde is a fashion editor favourite loved for its understated ballet flats – however, its block-heel sandals are some of the best around. This cream Natania style is perfect for summer events. Keep styling simple with a classic slip dress and let the tie-ankle detailing do all the talking.

    René Caovilla

    If you want a pair of powerful party shoes but can't fathom a night of dancing in discomfort, swap statement heels for a bit of sparkle. René Caovilla's sandals are embellished all-over with crystals and have slender straps that wind around your ankles.

    Isabel Marant

    For a modern take on the classic gladiator sandal, look to Isabel Marant. These block-heels capture the brand's bohemian aesthetic perfectly with wide straps, gold studs and statement buckle detailing. Add a dose of edge to feminine looks by wearing yours with floral maxi dresses.

    9

    Aquazzura

    The minimalist straps balance the bold metallic leather on this pair of block heel sandals from Italian shoe label Aquazzura – so you can wear them with everything from workwear (think black midi skirts) to summer dresses.
