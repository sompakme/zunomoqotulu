    Few weeks after Rihanna called it quits with her rich Arabian lover, Hassan Jameel, fans cannot stop talking about her ex-lover, Chris Brown, and how he is smitten by her.

    Chris Brown and Rihanna's relationship ended a long time ago but the singer has always continued to speak highly of the songstress.

    Through an Instagram video he shared, it is always clear that Rihanna will always have a soft place in Brown's heart.

    Brown shared a video of himself smiling in an interview captioning it with words that show he is still in love.

    "When someone asks, after all this time, why are you still in love with her? My answer: Because time has no relevance when it is unconditional," wrote Brown.

    Although the New Flame hitmaker did not say who he was referring to, his fans are convinced he was referring to Rihanna.

    Brown and Rihanna started dating in 2008 and broke up in 2009 when he brutally attacked her.
