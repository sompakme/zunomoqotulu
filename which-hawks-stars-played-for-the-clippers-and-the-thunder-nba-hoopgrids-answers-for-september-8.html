    Answers to the September 8 NBA HoopGrids puzzle

    Luc Mbah a Moute, Brian Skinner, Mikki Moore and Zach Randolph are Cell 1 answers. They played for the LA Clippers and Sacramento Kings.

    Players with Kings-Thunder connections include Justin Jackson, Patrick Patterson, Justin Robinson and Reggie Evans. Current Kings forward/center Domantas Sabonis is most likely the popular answer to Cell 2 of the NBA HoopGrids.

    Bogdan Bogdanovic played his first three seasons in the NBA with the Sacramento Kings. De’Aaron Fox was one of his teammates during that span. More options include Bruno Caboclo, Buddy Hield, George Hill and Vince Carter. Any of them will fit well in Cell 3.

    Mike Scott, Lou Williams, Rajon Rondo and Danilo Gallinari are Cell 4 answers. They all played for the Atlanta Hawks and LA Clippers in their careers.

    For Cell 5, Nazr Mohammed, Mike Muscala, Al Horford, Gus Williams and Lenny Wilkens are great fits. They all donned the jerseys of the Hawks and OKC Thunder.

    Cell 6 is where John Collins, Tyrese Martin, Bruno Fernando and Solomon Hill fit best. They have played with Bogdan Bogdanovic in Atlanta. Trae Young and Dejounte Murray, the Hawks’ superstars, are the easy and obvious answers here.

    Wilson Chandler, Jerome Robinson, Montrezl Harrell, JaMychal Green and Rodney McGruder were some of Shai Gilgeous-Alexander’s former teammates with the LA Clippers. Other options for Cell 7 of the NBA HoopGrids include Boban Marjanovic, Patrick Beverley, Landry Shamet and Milos Teodosic.

    “SGA” has been playing for the OKC Thunder over the last four seasons. Some of the teammates he played with in Oklahoma are Aaron Wiggins, Jared Butler, Tre Mann, Luguentz Dort and Josh Giddey. They’re all Cell 8 answers.

    A few players have had the chance to play with Bogdan Bogdanovic and Shai Gilgeous-Alexander in the NBA. Garrett Temple and Vit Krejci are two of them. They are answers to Cell 9 of today’s grid.
