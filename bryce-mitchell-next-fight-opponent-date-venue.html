    Bryce Mitchell, the No.10 ranked featherweight contender in the Ultimate Fighting Championship, had just lost his undefeated record in his last outing. The 28-year-old American fighter has been a fan favorite since his octagon debut in 2018, and his mixed martial arts record stands at 15 wins and 1 loss.

    Fighting out of Texarkana, United States, Mitchell trains at his farm and sleeps inside a trailer. Hence, people like him for his unique lifestyle and for representing his American roots inside the octagon.

    Bryce Mitchell Next Fight - Opponent, Date, Location

    Bryce Mitchell is set to face Dan Ige for his next fight at UFC Fight Night: Fiziev vs. Gamrot at the UFC Apex, Las Vegas, United States, on September 23, 2023.

    Coming off a loss against Ilia Topuria at UFC 282, Mitchell is set to make his UFC return and regain his win streak. At the same time, Dan Ige is coming off a two-fight win streak against Damon Jackson and Nate Landwehr, respectively.

    Dan Ige is currently ranked as the No.12 contender in the featherweight division. Hence, it is a chance for both fighters to climb up the rankings table and try their luck at the featherweight title in the coming years.

    Earlier, Bryce Mitchell was scheduled to face Movsar Evloev at UFC 288 but pulled off due to a recurring back injury.
