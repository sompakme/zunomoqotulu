    What are the four standards of evaluation?

    Feasibility standards ensure that an evaluation will be realistic, prudent, diplomatic and frugal. Propriety standards ensure that an evaluation will be conducted legally, ethically and with due regard for the welfare of those involved in the evaluation, as well as those affected by its results.
