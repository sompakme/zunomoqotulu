    Kobe Bryant left behind an incredible legacy in the sport of basketball but when it came to his place in the sneaker world, he had a similarly large impact. While his efforts with Adidas left a lot to be desired, there is no denying that Bryant had some amazing shoes with Nike. Over the last few years, Nike has been honoring the ten year anniversary of specific Kobe models and in 2021, they will be doing that all over again with the Nike Kobe 6 Protro.

    Over the past few weeks, we have reported on a whole slew of new Kobe 6 Protro colorways that are supposed to be dropping next year. Most of this information has been coming from @soleheatonfeet and recently, they came through with more info as they revealed that Spring 2021 will see the return of the Nike Kobe 6 "POP."

    This sneaker has a white base with black and gold accents which makes it a decent colorway for all of you Lakers fans out there. If this isn't your style, well, there will clearly be a lot of other models to choose from next year.

    Either way, if you're a sneakerhead and a Kobe Bryant fan, 2021 is about to be a massive year.
