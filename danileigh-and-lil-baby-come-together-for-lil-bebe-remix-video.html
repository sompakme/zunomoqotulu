    Fresh off a sold out stint at New York City’s SOB’s, DaniLeigh has come through with a new offering in the form of a music video to accompany her “Lil Bebe” remix, featuring Lil Baby.

    Last year marked the arrival of the singer’s The Plan debut project, anchored by a successful tour run that is almost at its end. Previously, the project found supplement in clip’s for tracks like “Blue Chips” and “Cruz.”

    The new music video takes on a much simpler concept than the outing attached to the original song, and finds Baby and Dani in an industrial setting with a vintage car in the background with Dani’s own choreography taking center stage. Watch the full video up top.
