    Miley Cyrus's former home in the palm-tree-filled kingdom in Toluca Lake was listed on the market for just under $6 million. Miley, and her family, moved from their Tennessee farm to the Los Angeles mansion in 2007 when the actress starred in the Disney Channel sitcom Hannah Montana.

    Following the show, Miley also launched her music career while living in the home before listing it on the market for $5.995 million.

    The 8,700 square foot estate, created by esteemed Montecito architect Bob Easton, fits effortlessly into the vibrant  Toluca Lake neighborhood in the golden state. Its curated interiors reflect its effortlessly stylish So-Cal ambiance through its six bedrooms, seven bathrooms, including a master suite with an adjacent walk-in closet and rooftop terrace. 

    See: Living room ideas – clever ways to decorate living spaces

    Notable architectural features include a statement curved staircase constructed of wrought iron, and an ornate fireplace, also in the master bedroom. In a nod to its Tuscan influence, Bob designed a stucco-style facade with glamorous arched doors and windows that mirror traditional Italian architecture. 

    Meanwhile, downstairs, there is a spacious family room, home theatre, and a chef's kitchen with ogee-edged granite countertops. There is also a music room that was filled with guitars owned by Miley's father, the Country music singer Billy Ray Cyrus.

    Beyond its vine-covered walls and red barrel tile roof, the home's exteriors are equally as aesthetic – boasting several outdoor fireplaces that warm the Californian air during al-fresco dinner parties. 

    The verdant garden is blessed, perhaps unsurprisingly, with sweeping palm trees, a stunning swimming pool idea, and a custom-built hot tub. 

    Miley sold the home for $5.995 million with Thomas Atamian of Dilbeck Real Estate.

    Where does Miley Cyrus live now?

    Miley reportedly still lives in LA, in a Hidden Hills mansion which she purchased for $4,953,500 in June 2020. However, the Tennessee-born star also has a house in  Nashville, which she bought in 2017 for approximately $5.8 million.
