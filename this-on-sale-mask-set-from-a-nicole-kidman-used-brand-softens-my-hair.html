    Vegamour, specifically its Hydr8 collection, has restored silkiness to my hair — and my faith in hydrating shampoos as a whole. I’m hardly alone in my love for the brand. Nicole Kidman, a Vegamour ambassador, calls its GRO Revitalizing Shampoo her “favorite.” Other honorable mentions: the best-selling, drug-free hair growth serum shoppers swear by, along with its nourishing Hydra8 Deep Moisture Hair Repair Mask and accompanying shampoo and conditioners.

    Best of all, the aforementioned hair saviors are sold, conveniently, in bundles, which are up to 35 percent off. Hurry — the sale won’t last forever.

    Best Vegamour Deals:

    Vegamour Gro Hair Serum Three Pack

    A true icon in the ever-widening hair-growth category, Vegamour’s serum is among the first I’d noticed garnering mass acclaim — and for good reason. Shoppers swear the lightweight, leave-in serum truly does what it claims. According to one reviewer, who struggled “for many years” with hair thinning, the serum has fully regrown “hair on the top of [their] head.” Another reviewer dubs it “a miracle” for promoting hair density; “my hair is growing faster, too,” they add. Another says the formula has made their hair “fuller” and “healthier.” 

    Notably drug-free and plant based, the serum contains scalp-stimulating caffeine, protein-rich mung bean, and biotin as key actives. For even speedier results, try the brand’s advanced serum formula, which contains antiinflammatory CBD to promote optimal scalp health.  

    Vegamour Shampoo and Conditioner Set

    Notably gentle, this in-shower cleansing set delivers a fresh feel without stripping strands of moisture. Formulated with the brand’s vegan keratin, the densifying duo strengthens weak strands, in turn reducing shedding over time. Instantly apparent post-shower? Softness, shine, and a delicate scent. 

    Vegamour HYDR-8 Essentials Kit

    This rich, hydrating hair mask and its accompanying shampoo and conditioner are formulated to quench even the driest tresses — and delivers on its claim, as I can personally attest. The indulgent cream softens my hair in minutes — even when it's haylike. Plus, it smooths straggling strands, leaving them softer and shinier after a single use. Vegan keratin strengthens weak, breakage-prone hair, while moringa oil replenishes lost moisture for noticeable shine and softness post-use. Unlike many other hair masks I’ve tried, Vegamour’s is devoid of a greasy, weighty feel.
