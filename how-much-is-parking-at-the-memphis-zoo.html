    So You Want to Visit the Memphis Zoo? Here's the Dirt on Parking (Literally)

    The majestic Memphis Zoo! Home to fascinating creatures, from playful penguins to grumpy grizzly bears (we've all been there, grizzly bear), it's a place where wonder and wildlife collide. But before you unleash your inner zoologist, there's one crucial question that needs answering: how much will it cost to park your chariot (or minivan, no judgement)?

    Buckle Up, Animal Adventurers: A Breakdown of Parking Costs

    Fear not, fearless zoo-goer! Parking at the Memphis Zoo is a chill $10. That's a small price to pay for a day of unforgettable animal encounters (unless you accidentally step on an ant pile, but hey, that's part of the adventure, right?).

    But Wait, There's More! Free Parking Perks

    Now, if you're rocking a Memphis Zoo membership (major props to your dedication to the animal kingdom!), then parking is completely free for you, my friend. Membership? More like "free-ship" for your car!

    Special Shoutout to Our Physically Challenged Pals

    The Memphis Zoo is all about inclusivity, and that extends to parking. Disabled parking is available in the main lot, and it's free of charge. Just remember to have your state-issued hangtag or license plate ready to show.

    How to Avoid Saying "Hey, That Wasn't There Before!" When You Get Your Bill

    • Don't confuse zoo parking with zoovenirs: Those plushie pandas ain't part of the parking fee, folks!
    • Time travel is tricky: This guide applies to present-day Memphis Zoo parking fees. If you're arriving in a DeLorean, double-check rates for your specific time period.

    Bonus Tip: Carpooling is a fantastic way to save some green (both cash and eco-friendly kind) and bond with your fellow zoo enthusiasts.

    How To FAQs:

    How to score free parking at the Memphis Zoo? Become a Memphis Zoo member! Your car will thank you.

    How much is disabled parking at the Memphis Zoo? Nothing! Disabled parking is free, but you do need to have a valid state-issued hangtag or license plate.

    How to avoid parking fees altogether? This might be a tricky one, but hey, public transportation or good old-fashioned shoe leather are always options! (Though, we wouldn't recommend rollerblading through the zoo exhibits.)

    How much does it cost to park a spaceship at the Memphis Zoo? Unfortunately, interstellar parking rates haven't been established yet. But hey, if you're rolling up in a spaceship, parking fees are probably the least of your concerns!

    How to guarantee a close parking spot? There's no guaranteed close parking, but arriving early can increase your chances. Alternatively, you can channel your inner zen and embrace the walk from the outer lots. It's a great opportunity to stretch your legs and get prepped for a wild adventure!
