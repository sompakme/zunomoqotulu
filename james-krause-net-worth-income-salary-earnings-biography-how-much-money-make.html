    James Krause (born 9 January 1987 in Bury St Edmunds, Suffolk, England) is a former professional footballer who is now a member of the Cambridge City team in the English Premier League. In his early career, Krause played as a defender for Ipswich Town, and he has also represented England at the under-17 level. On November 21, 2006, Krause agreed to a two-month loan contract with Carlisle United, with the possibility of a longer-term agreement. His loan agreement with Ipswich was renewed on 11 January 2007 until the conclusion of the 2006–07 season, however he returned to the club on 3 May 2007 after appearing in just three Premier League games with the first team. The Ipswich Town midfielder was released in May 2007 and went on to join Rushden & Diamonds, however he was released by the club again in August 2007 and went on to join Crawley Town.
