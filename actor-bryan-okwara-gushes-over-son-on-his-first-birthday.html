    Nollywood actor, Bryan Okwara, recently had reasons to celebrate as his son turned a year old. The proud dad took to his Instagram page to mark the day with a cute photo.

    Bryan accompanied the photo with a beautiful and intimate message for his little boy. The film star prayed to God for wisdom and protection for him.

    He wrote:

    "I have written several letters for your birthday and still. Words fail me to describe how much of a true blessing you are. At a point, hearing you cry as a child became so beautiful. Now I see why God loves children because Your blessings humble me. I pray that God fills your heart and days with joy and laughter. I pray that God grants you grace and the strength you need to pursue the things that you desire in life. I pray that God grants you wisdom and understanding as you grow older and begin to make choices of your own. I pray that God heals you for every time you fall and bruise your knees and elbows."
