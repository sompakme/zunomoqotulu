    Got money burning a hole in your pocket for your loved one? If you’re shopping for someone who loves the finer things in life (regardless of the price tag), we’ve rounded up the best luxury gifts both priceless and spendy for Mother’s Day, Father’s Day, graduation and other occasions.

    There’s something for every type of giftee. Mom might want that high-tech hair tool loved by Hollywood hairstylists, while your favorite fashionable foodies and busy techies may be eyeing a new smart appliance or accessory. If you’re shopping for the best gifts for kids, there are even some stylish and cool picks for them — say, their very own Maserati or a little something that makes them feel like a star.

    Related Stories

    Ahead, we’ve rounded up some of the best luxury gifts from Amazon (yes, you read that right), Bloomingdale’s, Mr. Porter, Neiman Marcus, Net-A-Porter, Nordstrom, Saks Fith Avenue and more retailers. (Bonus: not everything costs an arm and a leg.)

    For more of the best gift ideas, check out The Hollywood Reporter‘s shopping guides here.

    Urban Stems

    Urban Stems Luxe Flower Subscription

    One of the best online flower delivery services, Urban Stems lets you send your loved ones luxurious bouquets once or twice a month. Choose from three tiers ranging from $55 to $105 per delivery; the company does the rest of the work by curating every arrangement. Recipients receive a vase with their first delivery, and Seasonal and Luxe subscriptions include a candle with the first bouquet.

    MasterClass Subscription

    You don’t have to spend a fortune to gift the luxury of having some of the world’s smartest, most creative minds within arm’s reach, thanks to MasterClass — and you only have until today to get its two-for-one membership deal. The star-powered online learning platform lets members get business advice from Martha Stewart (MasterClass’ newest instructor), pro acting tips from Oscar-winning star Natalie Portman, sharpen their writing under screenwriter Shonda Rhimes, hone their debate skills with astrophysicist Neil deGrasse Tyson and score pro culinary pointers from Gordon Ramsay, to name just a few. And there are even more influential instructors across Hollywood, politics, fashion, business and more who share their expertise.

    Dyson

    Dyson Airwrap

    For the beauty buff who loves to be their own glam squad, Dyson’s latest version of its best-selling Airwrap multi-styler transforms flat tresses of all hair types and textures into red carpet-ready manes. (You can save up to $200 on refurbished models at Nordstrom Rack and Best Buy.)

    One of the best luxury beauty gifts that often sells out, the device comes with the Coanda smoothing drying, retooled 1.2- and 1.6-inch Airwrap barrels, firm and soft smoothing brushes and a round volumizing brush so giftees can curl, smooth, dry and wave their hair without leaving strands frizzy or damaged from heat. (Fans of Dyson’s high-end hair tools include starry hairstylists and brand ambassadors Jen Atkin and Matt Collins, celebrity makeup artist Priscilla Ono, Rosie Huntington-Whiteley and Jessica Alba.)

    Saatva

    Saatva Latex Hybrid Mattress

    What’s more luxurious than a great — and comfy — night’s rest? Saatva’s latex hybrid mattress is great for anyone with allergies and those who need extra back support. Five zones of latex provide pressure relief for the spine in any sleep position, while pocketed coils are responsive to movement and the body’s curves for ergonomic support. Made of temperature-regulating organic New Zealand wool, the breathable natural latex is certified by the Global Organic Latex Standard, Greenguard Gold and Eco-Institut, ensuring that the mattress is free of harmful emissions, chemicals and pollutants.

    Moon Boot

    Moon Boot Kids Nylon Boots

    For the stylish kiddos, Moon Boot’s lace-up footwear features a cushioned footbed, chunky silhouette and signature tassel and logo details. (The style has been worn by no shortage of stars, including Victoria Beckham, Paris Hilton, Dua Lipa and more.)

    PS

    PS Private Suite and Direct Service

    Learn more at PS

    The next time they’re leaving Los Angeles International (LAX) or Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International (ATL) airports, upgrade their travel experience with a private suite and direct service through PS. The luxurious private terminal serves commercial flights from both airports, allowing travelers to get the five-star treatment — even if they’re flying economy. They’ll get to ditch the main terminal and lines (thanks to PS’ private TSA and Customs and Border Protection screening), and drivers will escort them directly onto their plane.

    On top of all of that, PS’ lounges and private suites offer chef-prepared food, spa treatments and more. Suites start at $3,500 (for four people) and the optional All-Access membership is $4,850; learn more and get pricing here.

    The Extra Large EDITION Candle

    Take it from us: an oversized scented candle turns any “cozy space” into a luxury dwelling, and few candles are as chic as this one from the EDITION Hotel. The hand-poured candle features EDITION’s exclusive custom fragrance, which contains a blend of bergamot, black tea and cedar wood. Made in France, this is the same parfum that instantly envelopes you when you walk through the doors of the EDITION’s multiple properties. Now, you can bring it home with you, thanks to this four-wick, extra-large candle, which is crafted with black wax and housed in a sleek black ceramic glazed vessel. At just under five pounds, this is a weighty piece, but also a captivating one for all the senses. The candle has a burn time of approximately 184 hours.

A stylish towel and robe warmer, like this one from Zadro that holds up to two 40-inch-by-70-inch bath towels, or any other comfy essentials that recipients want ready for showers and chilly mornings. (It’s also on sale for over 40 percent off for a limited time.)

    Technogym

    Technogym Bench

    One of the best luxury gifts for high-tech fitness enthusiasts, Technogym’s home workout bench includes everything they need to build strength, stay flexible and sculpt their body. It includes five pairs of dumbells (5, 10 and 20 pounds), three elastic bands (light, medium and heavy), three pairs of weighted knuckles) a workout mat and a storage bench. Users can sync up with Technogym’s app for guided workouts, too.

    Chalonne

    Chalonne Palladio Apple Watch Band

    Upgrade their Apple Watch with Chalonne’s bejeweled leather Palladio bands that are finished with 14-karat white gold and black enamel triangles and white topaz gemstones. Compatible with all Apple Watch Series and sizes, the straps come in a wide range of colors and hardware options to suit their style.     Ring Concierge Diamond Roman Numeral Personalized Ring

    Tumi x McLaren Aero International Expandable 4-Wheeled Carry-On

    Nothing says luxury quite like Tumi’s collection with McLaren. The Aero International expandable carry-on from the line nods to the luxe automaker’s supercars, as seen in the durable CX6 carbon fiber shell, the sleek silhouette, compression straps inspired by the six-point harness and smart organization (such as the removable suit section, interior hanger bracket, front-lid or split-case main entry compartment and built-in USB port).

    $2,720.90$3,999.9932% off

The 98-inch (yes 98-inch) LED TV delivers true big-screen viewing from the comforts of home, and it delivers big features too, with a wider color gamut, impressive clarity and surprisingly robust, 3D-like sound.     Redwood Outdoors Thermowood 4-Person Cabin Outdoor Sauna

    Stars such as Chris Hemsworth, Lady Gaga, David Beckham and Amanda Seyfried (as well as no shortage of celebrity trainers) have shared their love for sauna sessions and the wellness benefits ranging from detoxification to boosting workout endurance. Whether they’re a fitness enthusiast or spa regular, treat your giftee to the luxury of an R&R haven in their own backyard with Redwood Outdoors’ cabin sauna.

    Made of Scandinavian Thermowood and equipped with a Finnish-made Harvia sauna heater (which the brand says is considered “the industry gold-standard”), this four-person outdoor sauna features two-level seating for moving between high and low heats. Self-assembly is required, but you can gift them a Task Rabbit service so they can let a pro do the hard work.

    Ecovacs

    ECOVACS Winbot W2 Omni Potable Window Cleaning Robot

    One of our top picks from CES 2024, Ecovacs’ upgraded Winbot W2 Omni window cleaning robot will keep your giftee’s glass looking crystal clear. The latest generation of the Winbot is more portable and quieter than its predecessor and now comes with an all-in-one station. On a demo window at CES, it easily suctioned onto the glass and moved in an intuitive path while cross-spraying for thorough cleaning. The microfiber pad left the surface streak-free and clear, too, and it can be set to one of four modes: fast, deep, spot or custom cleaning.

    When it comes to frameless glass (such as a shower wall or door), the Winbot easily detects edges for precise cleaning. Ecovacs says it can clean indoors and outdoors; it’s also got a safety carabiner and tether just in case the robot disconnects from the glass. Of course, you can also control it remotely with the company’s app.

This set of four crystal tumblers lets recipients sip in style, whether it’s their morning coffee or weeknight cocktails.

    Ohdoki The Oh!

    Spice things up in the bedroom (or wherever you do it) with Ohdoki’s The Oh! vibrator, one of our favorite finds at CES 2024. You can customize your experience with your own patterns, too.

    Ooni Volt 12 Electric Pizza Oven

    For the nights when they want to ditch delivery and whip up mouth-watering, chef’s-kiss-worthy pizza in less than two minutes, give your favorite gourmand Ooni’s Volt 12 electric pizza oven (wood pellet, gas and propane options also available), which recently made Oprah Winfrey’s 2023 Favorite Things list. The built-in handles also make it convenient for moving from the kitchen to the patio.

    David Yurman Buckle Classic Cable Bracelet

    TheraGun Pro Plus 6-in-1 Deep Tissue Percussion Massage Gun

    Therabody’s newest Theragun Pro Plus massage device now comes with heat and vibration therapy in addition to five attachments for targeted muscle relief — making it one of the best luxury gifts for athletes or anyone who could use supreme relaxation.     2024 Mercedes-Maybach EQS 680 SUV

    If they’ve worked hard all their lifetime (and could use a design-driven, electric-powered upgrade) and a Porsche isn’t their preference, go for Mercedes-Benz. The German luxury auto company’s Mercedes-Maybach EQS 680 SUV is the first all-electric model in its most luxurious series, the EQS SUV certainly lives up to Wilhelm Maybach’s belief that “what is good must also be beautiful.” The vehicle boasts an all-digital cockpit, personalization options for drivers and passengers in every leather seat, near-silent torque for all four wheels, a smooth and responsive drive that can go from zero to 60mph in a little over four seconds and a battery that charges from 10 to 80 percent within 30 minutes, to name just a handful of high-end features — making it the ultimate luxury gift for adventurers who love the finer things in life.

    Blacklane

    Blacklane Chauffeur Service

    For the frequent traveler, peace of mind is one of the best gifts. Get them safely from the tarmac to their dreamy destination (or home after a long day in the air) with a pre-booked private car from Blacklane, which has a global carbon offset program. The company’s iOS and Android app makes it easy to hail a ride or schedule one in advance, and its professional chauffeurs will treat your giftee to a five-star experience with door-to-door pick-up and drop-off in cities such as New York, Los Angeles, London, Dubai, Santa Barbara, Palm Springs, Miami and beyond. You can also hire chauffeurs by the hour and book seamless airport transfers (drivers will track your flight and wait up to one hour for free). The best part? Pricing is all-inclusive, so no need for your recipient to bring cash for tips.
