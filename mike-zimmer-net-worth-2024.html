    Early Life and Career Beginnings

    Zimmer’s NFL career began with the Dallas Cowboys in 1994, where he served as an assistant coach.     In 2008, Zimmer moved to the Cincinnati Bengals as the defensive coordinator. His strategies and leadership significantly improved the team’s defensive performance, earning him the NFL Assistant Coach of the Year award in 2009.
