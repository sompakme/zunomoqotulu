    The top 12 contestants for American Idol 2021 have been chosen, and here’s a look at how to vote for your favorite contestant as we explored the text codes for voting.

    The present season of American Idol has had some shocking moments so far. Viewers were left speechless after they found out Wyatt Pike dropped out of the show. To add to this, Luke Bryan revealed he had been tested positive. Despite this, the Top 12 contestants seem promising, and here’s how you can help them out by voting.

    View Instagram Post

    American Idol 2021 vote text codes explored

    You can vote for your favorite contestant using text. Just follow the steps mentioned below.

  • Find out the corresponding number of the contestant you would like to vote for.
  • Type the number of the contestant and send the message to 21523.

    • The number for each contestant will be shown when they perform on stage. Make sure to pay close attention to it. You can vote up to 10 times for your favorite contestant.

    What are the other ways to vote?

    American Idol offers two other ways for you to cast your vote. The first method is via their website idolvote.abc.com. You can go to the website and vote for your favorite contestant.

    The second method is voting through the American Idol app. You just need to download the app via the Play Store or Apple Store for this. Apple users can download the app here. Meanwhile, Android users can download the app here.

    It is important to note that only people located in the U.S., Puerto Rico, or the Virgin Islands can vote via these two methods. At the same time, you need to be at least 13 years old to vote.

    View Instagram Post

    How many times can you vote?

    The show allows you to cast 10 votes using one method. That is, you can send 10 votes via text message, 10 votes using the American Idol app, and 10 votes through their website.

    A total of 30 votes can be cast by you. At the same time, for the website and the app, you can reallocate your votes until the voting window closes. Just make sure to hit “save” if you happen to change your vote.

    Who are the top 12 contestants on American Idol?

    The top 12 contestants on the show are Alyssa Wray, Ava August, Beane, Caleb Kennedy, Casey Bishop, Cassandra Coleman, Chayce Beckham, Deshawn Goncalves, Grace Kinstler, Hunter Metts, Madison Watkins, and Willie Spence.
