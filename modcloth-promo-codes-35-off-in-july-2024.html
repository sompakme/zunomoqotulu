    FAQs

    Can I get free shipping at ModCloth?

    Economy shipping takes approximately 8-14 business days and is free on all orders over $150. For orders under $150, economy shipping costs $12.95.  Standard shipping takes approximately 2-5 business days, and is $2.49 for orders over $150, and $15.45 for orders under $150. If you’d prefer a more speedy delivery, you can opt for expedited shipping which takes approximately 2-3 business days, and costs $34.99, or next-day shipping, which takes approximately 1 business day and costs $49.99.

    Does ModCloth accept returns?

    If you’re not satisfied with your purchase from ModCloth, they have a 28-day returns policy. This means that you’ll have up to 28 days after your shipping date to return your purchase. 

    Can I shop the ModCloth sale?

    ModCloth runs sales during seasonal periods such as Christmas and Black Friday, but you can also shop the sale all year round by heading to the 'Sale page on the ModCloth website where you can make savings of up to 80%. 

    Does ModCloth have an email newsletter offer?

    Yes - simply enter your email address into the email pop-up box at the footer of the homepage and you will receive an exclusive 20% discount code to use on your next order. Not only will you be able to make some excellent savings, but you'll also stay up to date about the latest news, sales, product launches, and offers. 

    Does ModCloth have a rewards program?

    Join ModSquad rewards to earn and redeem points online. It's completely free to sign up and will see you earn exciting rewards and gifts when you shop online, such as an exclusive birthday gift, early access to exclusive deals, double and triple points days, early access to collection drops, and more.
