    BBNaija Lockdown star, Prince, is no doubt one of the most loved housemates in the Lockdown edition. His charisma and charm have won the hearts of many people.

    Prince made it known during the premiere of the BBNaija show that he comes from a royal family.

    Well, for those still in doubt, Prince is the son of a wealthy King.

    According to InfoNg, his father is King Leomike Enwerem who holds the title of HRH Eze Ebii 1 of Ebie land in Imo State. This makes him the Prince of Ebie in Imo state.
