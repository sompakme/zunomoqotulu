    Azealia Banks has caused many a controversy through her lack of social media filter. Banks has used her account to start beef with countless artists and celebrities such as Baauer, Perez Hilton, Angel Haze, Rita Ora and most recently A$AP Rocky. It appears that Azealia's label, publicist, or someone with some authority and common sense has pulled the twitter account out of the rappers hands.

    As we've seen with other twitter loudmouths, all of Azealia's tweets disappeared earlier today, and so far all tweets since have been strictly content based. The takeover was foreshadowed by the rapper's tweet on friday in which she revealed “My days of twitter terror are about to be over. I have to turn over my password.”
