    Editor-Loved Amazon Skincare

    Farmacy Green Clean Cleansing Balm 

    Until I met the $24 Farmacy Green Clean Cleansing Balm, I dreaded taking off my makeup at the end of every day. Other formulas I’ve used strip and irritate my sensitive skin, cloud my eyes, and barely budge my waterproof products. The Farmacy pick, however, melts makeup away with ease, leaving my skin feeling fresh and hydrated. Since ditching harsh wipes and removers, I’ve noticed a huge difference in my complexion — it’s softer and plumper after use. To experience the balm’s impressive results yourself, massage it into dry skin, rinse it with water, and wipe away any excess product with a gentle washcloth — you can thank me later. 

    Cosrx Snail Mucin Power Repairing Essence

    My skin has never looked better since adding the Cosrx Snail Mucin Power Repairing Essence to my daily regimen. On sale for $13 — an entire 50 percent off its usual $25 price tag — the serum deeply moisturizes and plumps your complexion. It’s formulated with a slew of skincare superstars, notably its hero ingredient, snail mucin. While it may sound questionable, snail secretion has major skincare benefits; it deeply hydrates, soothes irritation, and promotes collagen production to reduce visible signs of aging. Not to mention, Emily Ratajkowski is a fan of the formula, too, and more than 100,000 people have purchased it in the past month alone, per Amazon.  

    Sunday Riley Auto Correct Eye Cream

    Speaking of celebrity-approved skincare, the Auto Correct Eye Cream from Sunday Riley, one of Oprah’s favorite brands, is a top pick in my beauty arsenal. As someone with dark under-eye circles and pesky bags, the on-sale product is my hack for looking well-rested and fresh-faced. It contains caffeine to reduce puffiness, leaving you with a smooth, lifted look while targeting fine lines and crow’s feet at the same time. For seemingly magical results, I apply one to two pumps of the nourishing pick to my eye area both in the morning and at night.
